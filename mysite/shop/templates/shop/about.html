My Cart

MYCART, is an Indian multinational technology company based in that focuses on e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence. It is considered one of the Big Four technology companies along with Google, Apple, and Facebook. MYCart is known for its disruption of well-established industries through technological innovation and mass scale. It is the world's largest e-commerce marketplace, AI assistant provider, and cloud computing platform as measured by revenue and market capitalization. MYCart is the largest Internet company by revenue in the world. It is the second largest private employer in the INDIA and one of the world's most valuable companies. MyCART is the second largest technology company by revenue. MYCART was founded by DAULESHWAR on September 28, 2019, in jamshedpur,Jharkhand. The company initially started as an online marketplace for books but later expanded to sell electronics, software, video games, apparel, furniture, food, toys, and jewelry. In 2015, MYCART surpassed Walmart as the most valuable retailer in the INDIA by market capitalization. In 2017, MyCART acquired Whole Foods Market for $13.4 billion, which vastly increased MyCart's presence as a brick-and-mortar retailer. In 2018, Daule announced that its two-day delivery service, had surpassed 100 million subscribers worldwide.
